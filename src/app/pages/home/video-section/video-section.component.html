Event Overview

The Sikkim Rural Tourism Meet (SRTM) 2025 was hosted in the picturesque village of Uttarey, West Sikkim. It represented a significant leap forward in sustainable and community-based tourism.

Organized under the Sikkim INSPIRES project by the Tourism and Civil Aviation Department, it welcomed rural entrepreneurs, government officials, and tour professionals to collaborate on eco-tourism initiatives.

Highlights included immersive homestays, traditional food showcases, and cultural performances. Strategic alliances with travel influencers helped spread the message of Sikkim's rural richness nationwide.
