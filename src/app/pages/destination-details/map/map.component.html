How to Reach Rumtek Monastery

By Air

The nearest airport to Rumtek Monastery is Pakyong Airport (PYG), located about 35 km away. Another option is Bagdogra Airport (IXB), about 125 km away.

By Train

The nearest major railway station is New Jalpaiguri (NJP), about 120 km from Gangtok. From there, you can hire a taxi to reach Rumtek.

By Road

Rumtek is located around 23 km from Gangtok. You can hire taxis or take shared cabs from Gangtok. The roads are hilly and scenic, offering a beautiful journey.
