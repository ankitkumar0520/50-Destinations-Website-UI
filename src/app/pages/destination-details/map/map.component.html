How to Reach London

By Air

London is served by six major airports:

  • • Heathrow (LHR) - 24km west
  • • Gatwick (LGW) - 47km south
  • • Stansted (STN) - 64km northeast
  • • Luton (LTN) - 56km northwest
  • • London City (LCY) - 14km east
  • • Southend (SEN) - 64km east

By Train

Major train stations in London:

  • • King's Cross - Eurostar from Europe
  • • Paddington - Heathrow Express
  • • Victoria - Gatwick Express
  • • Liverpool Street - Stansted Express

By Road

Major highways connecting to London:

  • • M25 - London Orbital Motorway
  • • M1 - North to Leeds
  • • M4 - West to Wales
  • • M11 - Northeast to Cambridge
